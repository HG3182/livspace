Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions, answered. Explore common queries about our interior design process, pricing, and more.

What interior design services do you offer?

We provide residential and commercial interior design, space planning, 3D visualization, furniture selection, and styling services.

How does the design process work?

The process includes consultation, concept development, design presentations, revisions, procurement, and final installation.

Do you work with specific budgets?

Absolutely! We tailor designs to your budget and offer suggestions to get the best aesthetic value within your range.

How long does a typical project take?

Project duration varies by scope, but most residential projects are completed within 4–10 weeks from approval.

Can I see a design before committing?

Yes, we provide mood boards, 2D layouts, and 3D visualizations so you can see and approve the design before implementation.

Do you offer site visits?

Yes, we conduct on-site visits for measurements, planning, and quality checks throughout the project timeline.
